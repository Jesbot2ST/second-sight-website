Never Sit Still

Sydney, Australia

Fractional CTO Engagement

01 / Project Charter

Overview & Scope

Project Overview

With an 8-month backlog of stalled projects and urgent security requirements from eight major clients, the business requires fractional CTO leadership. This project will audit the environment, implement ISO 27001-aligned security, and unblock key vendor relationships to prepare the studio for international scaling in LA.

In Scope
  • IT audit and documentation
  • ISO 27001 gap analysis
  • 1Password / Bitwarden and Sophos rollout
  • SEO (Tangible) and Web (Ten Two) vendor coordination
  • Disaster Recovery playbook development
  • AI roadmap and execution plan
Out of Scope
  • MSP / Helpdesk support
  • Hardware and network repairs and maintenance
  • Creative production
  • Direct SEO content entry or website population
High-Level Timeline
Week 1
Audit of systems and vendors, workshops, contact SEO / web design / AI agency vendors
Week 2 Develop key deliverables, playback findings
02 / Objectives

What We're Building Towards

01
Establish Governance

Full visibility of architecture, assets, and vendor responsibilities. No more invisible dependencies.

02
Security & Resilience

Reach ISO 27001 readiness and move beyond the "backup-at-home" disaster recovery model.

03
Operational Decoupling

Transfer technical oversight from Directors to a formal governance framework. Free up leadership bandwidth.

04
Scalability

Build a waterproof infrastructure ready for the LA office expansion.

05
Strategy Review

Review the AI strategy and develop a roadmap and execution plan to integrate AI across studio operations.

03 / Delivery

Deliverables & Stakeholders

Key Deliverables — Phase 1
1.Hardware/Software Inventory and Application Architecture Diagram
2.Business Risk Register and RACI Matrix (ownership of tech tasks)
3.Recommended updates for Cybersecurity Policy and ISO 27001 gap analysis
4.Password Manager review and rollout strategy
5.Draft DR Playbook
6.12-month project plan
Stakeholders
M+R
Mike & Renee
NSS Directors — Project Sponsors and ultimate decision-makers
BR
Brett Raven
Fractional CTO — Strategic lead and vendor advocate
JB
Jesse Botella
Project Manager — Scheduling, communications, and task tracking
Governance
Weekly stakeholder meetings to monitor progress across all workstreams.
04 / Success Criteria

How We Know It's Working

ISO 27001 Security Controls

Documented evidence of security controls meeting client ISO 27001 requirements — unlocking the 8 major client relationships currently at risk.

Directors Out of Tech

Mike and Renee are no longer the primary contact for technical vendors. Oversight formally transferred.

Disaster Recovery Rehearsed

Successful end-to-end rehearsal of the new DR process — beyond the backup-at-home model.

100% Application Ownership

All core studio applications mapped with clear ownership in the RACI matrix. No application without an owner.
